Crucial subtlety in conveying a message, a statement, a phrase - melodic, lingual or formal, is consecutive replication of form of its
representation. But after conveying that, and apprehension in the subject's mind, the details of the construction are valueless, and
only the underlying meaning, for which it was constructed is carried within the subject. The meaning is extrapolated from the sequence
of representatives it is constituted with and the pre-existent perception of something closely resembling. Whereas inference, in
probabilistic sense, is definite (as in Dirac-delta of classical ones) only when the subject is wholly aware of the message it is
extrapolating the meaning from.
Take an instance of an essayist's mind while he's reflecting his ideas on paper to convey to the mass. The abstract form of the idea
somewhere in his mind, is first transformed into the motor-signals which then manifests in the muscular motion of his hand and gets
represented (by replication) on the paper. Since a graphic imprinted on paper doesn't vanish out on its own, that writing now is, at
first, spatiotemporally replicates its existence which wouldn't have been true unless the thought emerged in his head, and the decision
to jot that down. In the next phase, when reading it, the pattern of graphic sequence of symbols is replicated in the retina of another
likely subject and a neural cascade of avalanche correlates that to what he previously had in mind, which if were consistent to what
the essayist said, would assert reasonable comprehension in the reader's mind, and thus, another set of replication of message
followed. But notice that at this point, the reader, although acquired that knowledge from a symbo-graphic representation, doesn't
necessarily bother about the symbols or even that sequence they were arranged, as if it's job is done in that context and what's
important now is the essence that particular representation carried within. Nextly, when that reader conveys the matter to somebody,
his motor-signals now vibrate his vocal chord which in turn replicates the structure (essence) of that very first idea in the essayist's
head in the frequency distribution of nearby air-columns, which is again replicated in someone's eardrum and another process of
apprehension takes place, by virtue of reproduction of pattern in conveying an information. Note that: the replication in expressing a
message is straightforward and very minimal imagination can simulate the whole scene in a minute, but replication in extrapolating
meaning from message is more subtle and requires treatment of renormalization flow (*), where by innate property the subject's inner
structure is at the vicinity of the flow's fixed point enabling scalefree replication of external perturbation throughout its domain.
